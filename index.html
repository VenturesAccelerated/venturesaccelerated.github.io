Helping exceptional students to build stellar startup.

As a premier student business organization on campus, Ventures Accelerated helps empower the next generation of engineers, designers, and entrepreneurial managers. We work with extraordinary students, renowned professors, and distinguished sponsors to unite innovation, academia, and industry. Every year, our teams will get the opportunity to pitch their start-up for seed funding and present their idea to the world when ready.

No matter what phase of building your business you are in… Ventures Accelerated can support!

Our Success Philosophy


Mentorship

In order to cultivate future leaders and entrepreneurs, Ventures Accelerated values mentorship, which are seen through out its development series.

Challenging Assumptions

Ventures Accelerated seeks to view the world with a critical eye. While others see problems, we see opportunities to prove premises into the finale.

Community

Over the course of the school year, all members of Ventures Accelerated bond over multiple social activities - such as gatherings, banquets, and club trips.
